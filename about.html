Program Descriptions

  • Job Connection Center (JCC) - Help individuals attain employment, creating resumes, and introductions to a financial coach.

  • Career Path Program (CPP) - Assist clients aquire a certification for a new career through the TNAF (Temporary Assistance for Needy Families).

  • Bitwise - Introduces clients to the woprld of coding throgh a partnership with Bitwise.

  • Stay the Course - Help clients who are returning citizens with high recitivism rate get into a carreer, along with guiding them through the reentry process of acquring credentials such as birth certificats, state ID, reference for housing and a host of other services.

  • Reentry - As with the Stay the Course program all services are the same but it is offered to individuals with a low recitivism rate.
